About Tacloban City

Ormoc City is a third-class city located in the western coast of the island of Leyte, Philippines. It is known for its beautiful beaches, natural attractions, and thriving agricultural and fishing industries. The city is also a major economic center in the region, with a growing business process outsourcing (BPO) industry, as well as trade and tourism.

In World War II, Ormoc was the site of a major battle between American and Japanese forces. The Battle of Ormoc Bay, which took place in December 1944, was one of the largest naval engagements of the war in the Philippines. The city was heavily damaged during the battle and took many years to recover.
