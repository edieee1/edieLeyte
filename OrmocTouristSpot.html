Ormoc Tourist Spots

Lake Danao

Lake Danao National Park is a natural reserve located on the island of Leyte in the Philippines. The park is home to the eponymous Lake Danao, a picturesque and serene body of water surrounded by lush greenery. The lake is known for its clear, blue waters and is a popular spot for swimming, boating, and fishing.

One of the main attractions of the park is the lake's biodiversity. The lake is home to a wide variety of fish and other aquatic animals, including the endangered Danao Danao, a type of fish found only in the lake. The park is also home to a diverse range of plant life, including tropical rainforests, mangroves, and bamboo forests.

In short, Lake Danao National Park is a beautiful and peaceful destination for nature lovers, adventure seekers, and anyone looking to escape the hustle and bustle of city life. The park's natural beauty and diverse range of activities make it a must-see destination on any trip to Leyte, Philippines.

Alto Lake

Alto Lake is a man-made lake located in Ormoc City, Leyte, Philippines. The lake is a popular destination for locals and tourists alike, offering a wide range of activities and attractions.

One of the main draws of Alto Lake is its picturesque beauty. The lake is surrounded by lush greenery and offers stunning views of the surrounding mountains. Visitors can take a leisurely boat ride on the lake or rent a kayak to explore the lake's many inlets and bays. The lake also has a beautiful sunset that worth to capture.

Alto Lake is also a great destination for nature lovers, with a wide range of plant and animal life present in the area. Visitors can take a nature walk or hike in the surrounding forests and mountains, where they can see a wide variety of wildlife, including monkeys, birds, and butterflies.



Veterans Park

The Veterans Park in Ormoc City, Philippines is a beautiful and peaceful place to visit. It features a monument honoring the veterans of the Philippines, as well as a variety of trees and plants that provide shade and natural beauty. The park also has a playground for children and a gazebo for picnics and gatherings. It's a perfect place to spend a relaxing day with family and friends.

The park also has facilities such as benches and restrooms for visitors. It is often a location for patriotic events such as Veterans Day and Independence Day celebrations. The park is open to the public and is free to visit.
