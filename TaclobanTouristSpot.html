Tourist Spots

MacArthur Landing Memorial National Park

The MacArthur Landing Memorial National Park in Tacloban City, Leyte, Philippines is a must-visit destination for history buffs and nature lovers alike. The park is dedicated to preserving the memory of the historic landing of General Douglas MacArthur and the Allied Forces during World War II, which marked a turning point in the liberation of the Philippines from Japanese occupation. The park features a statue of General MacArthur, as well as a museum that houses artifacts and exhibits detailing the events of the landing.

But the park offers more than just historical significance, it is also a great place to experience the natural beauty of the Philippines. The park features lush tropical gardens, a mangrove forest, and a beach area, where visitors can enjoy swimming, snorkeling, and other water activities. The park also offers an ideal spot for bird watching, where visitors can see various species of migratory birds.

Santo Ni単o Shrine & Romualdez Museum

Visiting the Santo Ni単o Shrine & Romualdez Museum is a unique and enriching experience that combines history, culture, and religion. It offers a great opportunity to learn about the rich heritage of Tacloban City, the Philippines and its people. The Museum and Shrine offer a peaceful and serene environment, making it a great place to reflect, meditate, and connect with your spirituality. Overall, it is a must-visit destination for anyone interested in understanding the Philippines and its people on a deeper level.s

The Romualdez Museum, on the other hand, is a historical and cultural museum that showcases the rich heritage and history of the Romualdez family, one of the most prominent political and influential families in the Philippines. The Museum houses a collection of artifacts, photographs, and other historical items that provide a glimpse into the lives of the Romualdez family and their contributions to Philippine history.

Calvary Hill

Calvary Hill in Tacloban, Leyte is a must-visit destination for anyone looking for a spiritual and peaceful experience. The hill is a sacred site for Catholics, as it is home to a 14-station of the cross, depicting the Passion of Jesus Christ. Each station is marked by a statue of Jesus, Mary, and other figures of the Bible. It is a popular pilgrimage site and a destination for visitors looking for a place of contemplation and reflection.

Calvary Hill is also a popular spot for picnics and family outings, it is a great place to relax and enjoy nature with loved ones. It is also a great place for a workout, the hill has a set of stairs that can be a great exercise for those who would like to have a cardio workout.

Tacloban Church (Sto. Ni単o Church)

The Tacloban Church, also known as the Sto. Ni単o Church, is a must-visit destination for anyone interested in history, culture, and architecture. The church is one of the oldest and most historically significant buildings in Tacloban City, having been built in the 1700s and serving as a center of worship for the local community for centuries. The church has a unique architectural design, blending elements of both Spanish colonial and traditional Filipino styles

Visiting the Tacloban Church is an opportunity to learn about the rich cultural and religious heritage of the city and the region, as well as to appreciate the architectural beauty of the church. The church is also located in the city center and is easily accessible by public transportation, making it an ideal stop for those exploring the city.
