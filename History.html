Leyte is an island province in the Eastern Visayas region of the Philippines. The island has a rich history, dating back to the pre-colonial period when it was inhabited by various indigenous groups. During the Spanish colonization of the Philippines in the 16th century, Leyte was one of the first islands to be visited by Spanish explorer Miguel LÃ³pez de Legazpi.
