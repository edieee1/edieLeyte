Reasons to visit Leyte


Leyte offers a diverse range of natural attractions such as beautiful beaches, cascading waterfalls and lush tropical forests.

The island has a rich cultural and historical heritage, with various heritage sites, festivals and traditional crafts to discover.



Leyte is known for its warm and welcoming people, and visitors can experience the traditional hospitality and friendly local culture.
